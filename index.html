Cisco's Advance Lab Operations - CALO

Why intern at CALO

Looking for an internship to gain industry leading skills that are required in todays technical fields? Look no further than Ciscos Advanced Lab Operations Lab, where you will gain hands on experience working with Cisco equipment in a real world setting. To your left you can see CALOs mega lab located in RTP, NC. CALO has 11 sites world wide that offer internship roles, meaning you are not limited to where you want to do your internship. Cisco also provides a relocation bonus to help pay for moving if required. Working with Network Engineers on a daily basis help you to build your brand, making a good name for yourself is key to transitioning into a full-time position when the time is right
Responsiblities in CALO
Your daily responsiblities will include the following: the main role in CALO is building segments of customer networks for Network Engineers to troubleshoot issues that customers are facing in a non-production environment. Focusing on mainly layers 1 and 2 of the OSI model, interns cable networks according to provided topologies along with ensuring equipment is running the requested software. Other responsiblities included monitoring the case queue for high-priority cases that are time sensitive, providing lab infrastuture support, and training new interns on how to do the job.
